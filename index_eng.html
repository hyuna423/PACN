Service

Joint purchase and sale of work of art NFT

We want to open the way for ordinary people to become art owners with a small amount of money through the PACN Project NFT joint purchase platform in Korea.
Participants participate in the purchase and take ownership of a certain portion of the artwork, and this ownership can be verified through NFT authentication technology.

Service

Work of art trading and auction bidding

Art trades that transcend countries have problems such as fraud and forgery,
as well as currency exchange fees and exchange rate changes due to foreign currency transactions.
With PACN coin, you can solve currency exchange and foreign exchange transaction problems,
and you can easily proceed with overseas remittance.
By listing PACN coins on the global exchange and allowing users around the world to purchase and sell them,
it can have a feature that can be usefully used for global art trades and auctions between countries.

Service

Offline and online exhibition

The PACN project aims to continue exhibitions to the general public for the popularity of the art market.
Through the exhibition hosted by the PACN Project, many artists' artworks will be released to the public, thereby lowering the barriers to public access to art.
